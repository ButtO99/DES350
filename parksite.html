About the Park

From hiking to horseback riding, your outdoor adventures at Zion will be unforgettable. Zion’s 229.1 mi² of space to explore and rich history will make a trip your favorite in no time. The vast Zion Canyon may be the main focus, but don’t be afraid to check out its many rivers and smaller companion trail found in the Kolob Canyons. The diverse wildlife of Zion will also wow you on your travels. With safety measures in place, you will be sure to spot deer, falcons, eagles, and maybe even mountain lions!

Horseback Riding

A trail ride is an excellent way to explore the natural wonderland in and around Zion National Park. Let the horse do the work as it carries you into scenic areas away from the crowds of tourists. This is classic Western scenery and there is no better way to see it than on horseback.
