good design
is timeless
is notable
is convenient
is consistent
is concise
is adaptable
is innovative
is honest
is stylish
has purpose
It does not compromise its identity by conforming to the cultural norm of its time and manages to look good regardless of how long it has existed.
It has its own flair that distinguishes it from other designs without ruining its functionality.
Viewers or users will not have trouble understanding the purpose of the design because it works with them, not against them.
Its individual elements flow well together because they were made to work with each other.
It will never overwhelm its user and instead opt to simplify anything that is considered confusing and extraneous.
It understands where users will falter and works around common obstacles.
It paves the way for future designs because it pushes current boundaries further.
It never allows itself to be bland and lets style be as important as useability. Its creative yet also competent.
It never tries to trick the consumer into believing it’s something that it’s not.
It does not ever make the user question why it exists. Every element serves a purpose.
